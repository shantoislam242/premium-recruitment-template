Recruitment Flow

How We Serve Our Clients

A streamlined, transparent process to fulfill your manpower needs quickly.

1

Send Demand Letter

The employer issues a formal Demand Letter & Power of Attorney detailing the job categories, salary, and requirements.

2

We Shortlist Candidates

We advertise, source, and meticulously pre-screen candidates from our extensive database based on your criteria.

3

Trade Test / Interview

Shortlisted candidates undergo practical trade tests at our centers, followed by final interviews with the employer via Zoom or in-person.

4

Medical Clearance

Selected candidates complete mandatory health checkups through GAMCA or authorized medical centers.

5

Visa Processing

The employer provides the visa authorization. We manage all local documentation, embassy stamping, and BMET emigration clearance in Bangladesh.

6

Pre-departure Orientation

Workers attend mandatory briefing sessions to understand your country's laws, workplace ethics, and cultural norms.

7

Deployment

Flight itineraries are shared with you. Workers are safely deployed to your site ready to commence their duties.
