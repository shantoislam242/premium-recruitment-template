About Us

Who We Are

Government-licensed,
Top Manpower Agency.

Najifa International is a BMET registered top-tier recruitment agency in Bangladesh. Since 2018, we have been fully committed to ethical recruitment, connecting skilled and semi-skilled workers with premium opportunities across the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and beyond.

We don't just find jobs; we build careers and secure futures. We believe that everyone deserves a fair chance to improve their livelihood. That's why we maintain 100% transparency, fast visa processing, and complete step-by-step guidance from your first interview all the way to your deployment.

Alongside our esteemed sister concern, Dallas Overseas, we have built a trusted network of over 25 global clients, deploying thousands of successful workers who are now proudly supporting their families back home.
