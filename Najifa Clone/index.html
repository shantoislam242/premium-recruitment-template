FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to pay before getting the visa? +
No. We maintain full transparency in our payment process. You only pay at clearly defined stages and will never be asked for undisclosed fees. Our team explains every cost upfront.
Minimum educational qualification needed? +
For most job categories, SSC (Secondary School Certificate) is the minimum requirement. However, for many skilled/semi-skilled roles, we accept applicants based on their trade skills regardless of formal education.
Which countries and job sectors are available? +
We primarily place workers in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain and Malaysia. Job sectors include construction, engineering, catering, healthcare, driving, and general services.
What documents are needed to apply? +
You will need: a valid passport (2+ years validity), NID card, educational certificates (SSC minimum), 4 passport-size photos, and a medical certificate if available. We guide you through the full checklist.
Is training provided before departure? +
Yes. We operate 5 training centers that provide skill-based and pre-departure orientation training. This ensures you arrive abroad fully prepared and job-ready.
