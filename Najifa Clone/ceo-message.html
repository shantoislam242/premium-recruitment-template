Message from Our Founder & CEO

📸 CEO Formal Photo
A word from leadership

Mohammad Murad Umar

Founder & CEO, Najifa International

Welcome to Najifa International. For over a decade, we have been dedicated to bridging the gap between talented Bangladeshi workers and prestigious global employers. When I started this journey 12 years ago, my vision was simple yet profound: to create a fully transparent, ethical, and hassle-free pathway for our hardworking people to secure their futures abroad.

The manpower industry often faces challenges regarding trust and transparency. At Najifa International, we have actively worked to eliminate these barriers. We treat every applicant not just as a candidate, but as a member of our extended family. From the initial consultation to skill training, and from visa processing to their safe arrival at the destination country, we stand by their side.

"Our true success is measured not by the numbers we deploy, but by the lives we uplift and the families we secure back home."

To our esteemed global clients: we understand the value of a dedicated, skilled workforce. Our rigorous screening and trade-testing processes ensure that we deliver only the best candidates who will add true value to your operations.

Whether you are an individual looking to build a better life overseas, or an employer seeking reliable manpower, my team and I are here to serve you with absolute integrity and commitment.

M. Murad Umar
