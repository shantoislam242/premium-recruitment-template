How to Apply — Step by Step

We guide you through every step of your overseas journey, completely hassle-free.

Your Journey

The Path to Your Dream Job

1
Step 01

Register & Apply

Submit your basic details via our online form or visit our Banani office. Our counselors will assess your profile for free.

2
Step 02

Submit Documents

Provide your valid passport, NID, and other necessary paperwork. We ensure everything is perfectly aligned with international requirements.

3
Step 03

Interview & Trade Test

Depending on the job category, you will participate in an employer interview or a practical skills test at our training center.

4
Step 04

Medical Checkup

Complete your mandatory medical fitness test at GAMCA or other authorized medical centers required by the destination country.

5
Step 05

Visa Processing

Once medically cleared, we handle the legal complexities of your visa application, keeping you updated with 100% transparency.

6
Step 06

Pre-departure Training

Attend our BMET-mandated orientation to learn about the culture, laws, and basic language of your destination country.

7
Step 07

Fly!

Collect your flight ticket, visa copy, and emigration clearance. Board your flight and start your new life abroad!

Preparation

Required Documents

Keep these items ready to ensure a smooth application process.

✓

Valid Passport

Must have a minimum of 2 years validity remaining.

✓

National ID Card (NID)

Original copy and photocopies of your smart card or NID.

✓

Educational Certificates

SSC minimum for most roles. (Trade certificates if applicable).

✓

4 Passport-size Photos

Recent photos with a white background.

✓

Medical Certificate

Provide if you already have a valid clearance (otherwise we guide you).

✓

Experience Proof

Previous job letters or driving licenses (for skilled jobs).

No experience? No problem.

We provide comprehensive, skill-based training before your departure. Whether you need to learn construction, hospitality, or heavy driving, our 5 dedicated training centers will make you job-ready.
